Dining & Drinks


KLINX
Celebrate the finer moments in life with the 'clink' of glasses in Klinx. Offering live music and an inviting Old World ambience, this stylish lobby bar serves a variety of spirits - including the finest single malts and cognacs - wines, beers, and signature and classic cocktails. Klinx also offers 'test-tube'-style shots, subtle herb-infused spirits, and exotic, non-alcoholic drinks and mocktails and features a Humidor for tobacco enthusiasts.
Ministry of Food
With live interactive cooking stations and exquisite culinary creations, this all-day restaurant serves a wide selection of European, Pan-Asian, Indian, and Japanese delicacies. Complement your meal with a bottle of wine or choose from our specially crafted wine and food pairing menu for a complete dining experience. There is also a private dining space and an exclusive wine table for a select gathering.
54Thirty Rooftop
Voted #1 Best Bar in Denver (Society19). Experience a local favorite at Denver's highest open-air rooftop bar, perched 5,430 feet above sea level. Up here, the vibes are enhanced. The mood is stellar. The drinks even taste a little better. *Outdoor dining available all year weather permitting.
Recess
Recess serves freshly brewed tea and coffee as well as an extensive choice of cakes, hand-made chocolate delights, gourmet sandwiches, and pastries for dine-in or takeaway. Enjoy a cup of tea or garner an Illy experience, sharing stories and delicious food, with new friends at the community tables. The discerning tea drinker will find a range of perfectly brewed teas from signature world estates.
Ossiano
At Ossiano restaurant, fine dining is an immersive experience. Imagine feasting on a sumptuous dinner in one of Dubai’s most romantic underwater restaurants, as stingrays, sharks and fish glide right past your table. Make this anniversary, birthday, or any occasion their most memorable yet. We can arrange for one of our skilled divers to deliver a personalised message to your loved one.
Ronda Locatelli
Enjoy relaxed dining at this casual Italian restaurant in Dubai, where Michelin-starred Italian chef Giorgio Locatelli creates delicious antipasti, stone-hearth pizzas and the best fresh pasta in Dubai. With plenty of vegan and gluten-free options, outdoor dining and a playground for the kids, Ronda Locatelli is the perfect destination for families, friends and couples.
